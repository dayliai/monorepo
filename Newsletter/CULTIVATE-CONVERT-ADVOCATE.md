### Template A: Submit Your Challenge

**Subject:** We're building something new — and we need your story.  
**Preview text:** What daily living challenge should we tackle next?

---

Hi {FNAME},

You've been reading about solutions that work. Now we want to ask you something:

**What solution *doesn't* exist yet?**

At Daily Living Labs, we don't just share what's already out there — we build what's missing. Our "Build What Doesn't Exist" program connects your real-world challenges with university design teams, OTs, and engineers.

Here's how it works:

1. **You describe your challenge** — through our guided conversation (takes ~5 minutes)
2. **Our team reviews it** — we look for patterns and feasibility
3. **A design team picks it up** — students and researchers prototype a solution
4. **You get updates** — and first access when it's ready

Some of our current builds started exactly this way — a caregiver who couldn't find what they needed, so we built it together.

Your challenge could be the next one we tackle.

## Milestone-Triggered Advocate Emails

These are sent automatically based on account activity:

| Trigger | Email |
|---------|-------|
| User's submitted solution is published | "Your solution is live — here's who it's helping" |
| User's challenge gets picked up by a build team | "Great news — a team is working on your challenge" |
| 3 months since account creation | "Here's the impact you've had in 3 months" |
| User's referral signs up | "Someone you referred just joined — thank you" |
| User's referral submits a solution | "The ripple effect — your referral shared a solution" |

---

## The Loop Closes

When an Advocate's referral signs up, that new subscriber enters at **Capture** — and the infinity model continues.

```
ADVOCATE refers a friend
    ↓
Friend receives CAPTURE welcome series
    ↓
Friend progresses through NURTURE
    ↓
Friend participates in CULTIVATE
    ↓
Friend CONVERTs to active member
    ↓
Friend becomes an ADVOCATE
    ↓
ADVOCATE refers another friend...
```

*Track the chain:* Mailchimp's referral tags let you see multi-generational referral chains — Advocate A referred B, who referred C, who submitted a solution that helped 40 families.
