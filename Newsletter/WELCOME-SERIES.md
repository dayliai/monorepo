## Email 2: The ADL Explainer (Day 3)

**Subject:** What are ADLs? (And why they matter more than you think)  
**Preview text:** The 6 activities that define daily independence — and where help exists.

---

Hi {FNAME},

You've probably heard the term "Activities of Daily Living" — or maybe you haven't, and you just know that *something* about daily life has gotten harder.

Either way, here's what ADLs actually are:

**The 6 core Activities of Daily Living:**

1. **Bathing & showering** — getting clean safely and independently
2. **Dressing** — putting on and taking off clothing
3. **Eating & drinking** — feeding yourself at mealtimes
4. **Mobility** — getting from place to place
5. **Toileting** — using the bathroom independently
6. **Transferring** — getting in and out of bed, chairs, or a car

These aren't just clinical terms. They're the building blocks of independence — the things most people do every day without thinking about them.

**When one of these gets hard, everything gets hard.**

But here's what we've learned from our community: for almost every challenge, someone has found a creative, practical solution. Sometimes it's a $12 product from Amazon. Sometimes it's a DIY hack. Sometimes it's a technique an OT taught them.

**Our job is to find those solutions and share them with you.**

## Email 3: The Story (Day 7-10)

**Subject:** Rosa's mornings used to take 2 hours. Here's what changed.  
**Preview text:** One tool, one tip, and a completely different start to the day.

---

Hi {FNAME},

We want to introduce you to Rosa.

Rosa is 67 and lives with arthritis in both hands. For years, her mornings were a battle — buttons she couldn't fasten, jars she couldn't open, a shower she didn't feel safe in alone.

She thought that was just how life was going to be.

Then her daughter found a magnetic button conversion kit — a simple product that replaces the buttons on existing clothing with hidden magnets. Same clothes. No sewing. $15.

That was the first domino.

Through her OT, Rosa discovered a suction-cup grab bar that installs without drilling. Through our community, she found a weighted utensil set that made mealtimes independent again.

**Rosa's mornings went from 2 hours to 45 minutes.** Not because of one miracle product — but because the right small solutions found her at the right time.

That's exactly what Daily Living Labs is here to do.
